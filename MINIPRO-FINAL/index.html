Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by visiting the "My Orders" section in your dashboard. There you'll find the status of all your orders, including scheduled pickup dates and times.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all products in their original condition. Please visit our store with your receipt and the product you wish to return.

How do I schedule a pickup?

When you checkout, you can select a preferred pickup date and time. We'll prepare your order and have it ready for you at the scheduled time.

Do you offer loyalty programs?

Yes, we offer a loyalty program where you earn points with every purchase. These points can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases.
